Bugatti Veyron


Bugatti Veyron


The Bugatti Veyron 16.4 is a legendary, high-performance hypercar, renowned for its 8.0-liter W16 engine producing 1,000+ horsepower and speeds exceeding 400 km/h. Launched in the 2000s, it combined unparalleled speed with luxury, featuring a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission and all-wheel drive. It set milestones in automotive engineering with specialized tires, advanced braking, and immense power.
